About Food Nagri

Discover Bihar's Authentic Food Culture

Who We Are

Food Nagri is a modern food discovery platform that helps people explore the most famous dishes, restaurants, cafes, and street food across Bihar. Our goal is to preserve Bihar's rich food heritage while helping users discover authentic local flavors quickly and easily.

Our Mission

To connect every food lover with the authentic taste of Bihar and support local restaurants through technology.
